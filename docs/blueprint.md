# **App Name**: Strategia Libre

## Core Features:

- Service Catalog: Showcase detailed marketing services with descriptions and pricing. Services include: Brand Diagnostics, Content System, and Performance & Ads.
- Portfolio Showcase: Display previous projects with descriptions of the work done and results achieved.
- Client Testimonials: Display client testimonials to build trust and credibility.
- Contact Form: A contact form to allow potential clients to submit inquiries.  Captures name, email, company, and message.
- Landing Page Optimization Suggestions: Suggest landing page optimizations such as modifications to page layout, copy and the call to action for different audience segments using data gathered from user analytics and market analysis. Provides data-driven recommendations.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant Argentinian Blue (#00AEEF) with subtle gradients to deeper blue for actions and buttons.
- Accent color: Solar Gold for subtle icons and highlights (#FFD700).
- Background color: Soft Cream Beige (#F5F5DC) for the content column.
- Font: 'Montserrat', a modern sans-serif, bold and legible.
- Split-screen design for the hero section with images of Argentinian landscapes (Patagonia) in the left column, and the contact form in a floating white card on the right.
- Line icons for services to communicate their nature
- Parallax scrolling effect for the Solution section, featuring the Argentinian countryside.
